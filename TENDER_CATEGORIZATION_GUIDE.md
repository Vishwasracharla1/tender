# RAK Tender Categorization & Upload Guide

## How to Use the New Features

### Step 1: Access Tender Intake Page
1. Open the application
2. Click on **"Tender Intake"** in the left sidebar (first menu item)
3. You'll see the main Tender Intake & Validation page

### Step 2: Select Issuing Department
At the top of the page, you'll see the **"Issuing Department"** section with 5 RAK departments:

- **RAK Municipality** - Can issue all tender types (Works, Supplies, Services, Consultancy)
- **RAK Public Works Department** - Works and Consultancy only
- **RAK Healthcare Authority** - Supplies, Services, and Consultancy
- **RAK Police** - Supplies and Services
- **RAK Education Department** - Works, Supplies, and Services

**Action:** Click on any department card to select it. You'll see:
- The card border turns blue
- Blue background highlights the selection
- Allowed category badges are shown

### Step 3: Choose Tender Category
After selecting a department, the **"Tender Classification"** section appears with 4 main categories:

1. **Works & Construction**
   - Building Construction
   - Infrastructure Development
   - Renovation & Retrofit
   - Landscaping & Green Spaces

2. **Supplies & Equipment**
   - IT Equipment & Hardware
   - Office Supplies & Furniture
   - Medical Equipment & Supplies
   - Vehicles & Transport Equipment

3. **Services**
   - Maintenance & Facility Management
   - Cleaning & Sanitation
   - Security Services
   - IT Support & Software

4. **Consultancy & Professional Services**
   - Engineering Consultancy
   - Architecture & Design
   - Management Consultancy
   - Legal & Regulatory

**Action:** Click on a category card (e.g., "Works & Construction")

### Step 4: Select Subcategory
Once you select a category, subcategories appear below:

For example, if you selected **Works & Construction**, you'll see:
- Building Construction
- Infrastructure Development
- Renovation & Retrofit
- Landscaping & Green Spaces

**Action:** Click on a subcategory (e.g., "Building Construction")

### Step 5: Automatic Evaluation Criteria Capture
After selecting both category and subcategory, you'll see:

**"Auto-Captured Evaluation Criteria"** section with green success boxes showing:
- ✓ Technical Capability - Experience in construction, project management, safety record (Max: 100)
- ✓ Financial Stability - Financial strength, bonding capacity, credit rating (Max: 100)
- ✓ Past Performance - Track record, quality of previous work, references (Max: 100)
- ✓ Health & Safety - Safety protocols, accident record, HSE certifications (Max: 100)
- ✓ Quality Management - ISO certifications, quality control processes (Max: 100)

**Plus:** A message showing "✓ 5 evaluation criteria automatically configured"

**Action:** Click "Apply Configuration" button

### Step 6: Alert Confirmation
You'll see an alert popup showing:
```
Tender configured with 5 evaluation criteria!

Auto-captured criteria:
• Technical Capability
• Financial Stability
• Past Performance
• Health & Safety
• Quality Management
```

Click OK to dismiss.

### Step 7: Document Upload Section Appears
Now you can see:

**Left Column:**
- **Upload Documents** section with file upload dropzone
- **Validation Panel** with automated checks

**Right Column:**
- **Normalization Summary** showing data standardization
- **Vendor Error Heatmap** showing quality checks
- **Unit Harmonization Log** showing real-time conversions

### Step 8: Upload Tender Documents
In the **Upload Documents** section:
1. Drag and drop files OR click to browse
2. Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, XLSX
3. Files are automatically validated and processed
4. Watch the validation panel update in real-time

### Step 9: View Results
As documents upload, you'll see:
- **KPI Widgets** at the top update:
  - Bids Validated percentage
  - Average Validation Time
  - Total Errors count
- **Validation Panel** shows completion status
- **Vendor Error Heatmap** highlights issues
- **Unit Harmonization Log** shows conversions

### Step 10: Proceed to Evaluation
Once validation is complete:
1. Click **"Proceed to Evaluation"** button in the footer
2. The system carries forward all captured evaluation criteria
3. You'll be taken to the Evaluation Matrix page

## What Makes This Special?

### Automatic Configuration
- **Zero manual setup** - Just select department and category
- **Pre-configured criteria** - Based on UAE procurement standards
- **Consistent framework** - All tenders of same type use same rules

### RAK-Specific
- **Real departments** - Actual RAK government entities
- **Allowed categories** - Each department has specific tender types
- **Local compliance** - Follows UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 11 of 2023

### Smart Evaluation
Each tender category has professionally designed criteria:
- **Works**: Technical, Financial, Safety, Quality, Performance
- **Supplies**: Quality, Price, Delivery, Support, Reputation
- **Services**: Quality, Expertise, Cost, Response, Environmental
- **Consultancy**: Qualifications, Experience, Methodology, Innovation, Local Knowledge

## Troubleshooting

**Q: I don't see the Department Selector**
A: Make sure you're on the Tender Intake page (first item in sidebar)

**Q: Category selector doesn't appear**
A: You must select a department first

**Q: Upload section is hidden**
A: You must:
  1. Select a department
  2. Choose a category
  3. Pick a subcategory
  4. Click "Apply Configuration"

**Q: Can I change the department after selecting?**
A: Yes, just click a different department card. The category selection will reset.

**Q: What if I want different evaluation criteria?**
A: The auto-captured criteria are defaults. You can customize them in the Evaluation Matrix page after proceeding.
