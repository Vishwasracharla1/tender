## Key Metrics Explained

### Procurement Value (AED 847.2M)
- **What it means:** Total value of all tenders in selected period
- **Why it matters:** Indicates economic activity and budget utilization
- **Good trend:** Steady growth aligned with development plans
- **Red flag:** Sudden spikes or drops need investigation

### Active Tenders (142)
- **What it means:** Number of tenders currently in progress
- **Why it matters:** Shows procurement pipeline health
- **Good trend:** Stable with seasonal variations
- **Red flag:** Too many = bottleneck, too few = underutilization

### Vendor Participation (1,847)
- **What it means:** Unique vendors engaged with RAK
- **Why it matters:** Competition drives better value
- **Good trend:** Increasing diversity, strong SME growth
- **Red flag:** Declining participation or concentration risk

### Avg Evaluation Time (23 days)
- **What it means:** How long tender evaluation takes
- **Why it matters:** Speed of procurement delivery
- **Good trend:** Decreasing while maintaining quality
- **Red flag:** Increasing delays impact project timelines

### Compliance Score (94.7%)
- **What it means:** Adherence to procurement policies
- **Why it matters:** Legal and governance requirements
- **Good trend:** Above 90% consistently
- **Red flag:** Below 85% requires immediate action

### Cost Savings (AED 42.3M)
- **What it means:** Money saved vs initial estimates
- **Why it matters:** Efficient use of public funds
- **Good trend:** Consistent savings through competition
- **Red flag:** Negative savings = overpaying

### Transparency Index (97.2%)
- **What it means:** Public visibility of procurement process
- **Why it matters:** Citizen trust and accountability
- **Good trend:** Increasing transparency
- **Red flag:** Declining transparency hurts reputation

### Integrity Alerts (7)
- **What it means:** Potential fraud or compliance issues detected
- **Why it matters:** Protects against corruption
- **Good trend:** Decreasing alerts over time
- **Red flag:** Increasing alerts need investigation
